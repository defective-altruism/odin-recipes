Green Curry with Tofu.

From Choosing Chia: "This Thai Green Curry Tofu features crispy tofu tossed up in a luscious creamy coconut sauce, with some spicy green curry! It’s a simple and healthy recipe that’s bursting with Thai flavour!"

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu, pressed, and cut into cubes
  • 3 tbsp olive or vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • dash of cumin powder
  • dash of ginger powder
  • dash of dried basil leaves
  • 1 medium zucchini, chopped
  • 1 tbsp avocado oil
  • 1 jalepeno or chili pepper
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • One 14 oz can coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tbsp olive/vegetable oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook on each side for 3-4 minutes until crispy and golden brown.
  2. Move the tofu cubes to a bowl then heat the remaining 1 tbsp of olive/vegetable oil in the skillet.
  3. Add the onion, carrots, zucchini and cook for 2 minutes, then add the curry paste, jalepeno, soy sauce and garlic, and then cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add coconut milk and leave on simmer for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Add back the tofu to the skillet and add the cumin, ginger, and basil seasonings and let sit until everything is well combined.
  6. Adjust seasonings as needed.
  7. Enjoy!
